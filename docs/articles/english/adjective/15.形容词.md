## 特征性质
She has a `beautiful` voice.

He is a `smart` student.

My brother is `tall` and `strong`.

## 情感态度
I’m so `happy` to see you!

She was `angry` at the unfair result.

The kids were `excited` about the trip.

## 颜色、大小、形状
I bought a `blue` dress.

We live in a `small` apartment.

The table is `round`.

## 数量/程度
There are `many` books on the shelf.

He has `few` friends here.

Is there `enough` time?

## 国籍/材料/用途

She speaks `Chinese` fluently.

This is a `wooden` chair.

I need a `cooking` pot.

## 动词的第三人称单数形式
若句子的主语是第三人称单数,且句子时态为一般现在时,则谓语动词应用第三人称单数形式.

### s,x,ch,sh结尾
She `passes` the salt to me at dinner.

My dad `fixes` broken toys for me.

She `watches` the news every evening.

The child `brushes` his teeth before bed.
### y结尾
She `studies` biology at university.

My brother always `tries` his best.
> 辅音字母`+y`,`-y`+`ies`

### o结尾
She `goes` to school by bus every day.

My brother `does` his homework after dinner.

His voice `echoes` in the empty hall.

The president `vetoes` the bill.


## 动词的ing形式

### 一般情况
`Working` overtime is common in this company.

I enjoy `playing` the piano in my free time.
### 以不发音的e结尾
She is `writing` a novel about her childhood.

He apologized for `making` such a big mistake.
### 以重读闭音节结尾，且末尾只有一个辅音字母
`Running` every morning keeps me healthy.

They saw a shark while `swimming` near the reef.

The cat is `sitting` on the windowsill, watching birds.
### 以ie结尾
Stop `lying`! I know you broke the vase.

He spent five minutes `tying` his shoelaces.

## 动词的过去式和过去分词
### 一般情况
She has `played` the piano since she was five.

I have `lived` here since 2020.
### 以辅音+y结尾
We have `studied` English for six years.

She has `tried` many times to fix the problem.

### 双写辅音+ed
He has `stopped` smoking for two months.

The trip has been carefully `planned` by our teacher.

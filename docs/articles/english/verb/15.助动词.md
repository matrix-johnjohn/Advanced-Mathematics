### be
### 现在时态
I `am` a student.

The cat `is` sleeping.

You `are` my best friend.

### 过去时态
I `was` late for school yesterday.

You `were` right about the answer.

## have
We `have` a big house.

He `has` a new phone.

She `had` left when I called.

## do
I `do` my homework every evening.

He `does` the dishes after dinner.

`Did` they go to the party?

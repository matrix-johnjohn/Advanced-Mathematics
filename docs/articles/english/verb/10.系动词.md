## 状态系动词
She `is` a doctor.

They `are` happy.

The book `was` on the table.

I `am` tired.

## 感官系动词
You `look` pale. Are you okay?

That `sounds` like a great idea!

The flowers `smell` sweet.

This soup `tastes` salty.

The blanket `feels` soft.

## 保持
The weather `remained` cold all week.

Please `stay` calm.


## 似乎
She `seems` upset.

## 变化系动词
He `became` famous overnight

It’s `getting` dark.

Leaves `turn` yellow in autumn.

She `grew` impatient waiting for the bus.

Milk `goes` sour quickly in summer.

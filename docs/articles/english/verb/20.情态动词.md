## 常见情态动词的用法

### can,could

#### 表示能力或允许
He `can` speak Chinese very well.

You `can` have a holiday over Christmas.

#### 表示过去的能力或请求
Jack `could` swim when he was five years old.

`Could` I use your bike?

### may,might
#### 表示允许和可能性
You `may` leave now.

He `may` give up this chance.

#### 语气委婉
We `might` as well allow her to live here.

### mush,have to

#### 主观看法或肯定推测
I think you `must` buy something for her.

You `must` be joking.

#### 客观必须
According to the rule,you `have to` do so.
### should,ought to
#### 语气较轻
You `should` make plans for the future.

Every citizen `ought to` obey the law.
#### 语气较重

### will,would

#### 描述过去的习惯
We `would` attend lectures when we were at college.

#### 表示请求
`Will` you hand that book to me?
### used to

#### 描述过去习惯性的状态或动作
He `used to` play tennis in this gym.

I `used to` live in this city.
### need
He `needn't` come if he is busy.

Mary `needs` our help.

I `need` to get some sleep.
## 情态动词+Have
### 表示对过去情况可能性的推测
Tom `may have` met her friend.

### 表示对过去情况的肯定推测
She `must have` got up,because the light in her room is on.

### 表示本能够做某事
We `could have` made better plans.

### 表示本该做某事
I `should have` helped you.

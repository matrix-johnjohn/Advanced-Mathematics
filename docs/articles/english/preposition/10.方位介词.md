## 二维位置关系

### 表示在某地范围内
Shanghai is `in` the east of China.

### 表示与某地相邻接壤
Vietnam lies `on` the south of China.

### 表示在某地范围外
Japan is `to` the east of China.

## 三维上下关系

### 上方
#### 指物体的正上方且与其接触
There is a cup `on` the desk.

#### 指物体的正上方且与不其接触
There was a lamp hanging `over` the table.

#### 指位置高于物体
The plane flew `above` the city.

### 下方
#### 指物体的正下方且与其接触
The book was hidden `beneath` a pile of clothes.

#### 指物体的正下方且与不其接触
A boy is standing `under` the tree.

#### 指位置低于物体
The sun sank `below` the horizon.

## 前方
### 指某一空间外的前面
`In front of` my home runs a small river.
### 指某一空间内的前面
John sits `in the front` of the classroom.

## 空间关系
### 从表面横穿
The Great Wall runs `across` the mountains.

### 从内部穿过
The train goes `through` the tunnel.

### 从旁边经过
He walked `past` me without saying hello.

### 翻越后到达另一侧
She climbed `over` the wall.

## 范围
### 两者之间
`Between` Rome `and` Paris.

### 多者之间
`Among` middle school students.

## 人

### 主语
`Who` is your IELTS teacher?


### 宾语
`Whom` did you invite to the lecture?

To `whom` should I address this letter?

### 表所属关系
`Whose` idea was it to start the project?

## 二级标题

### 什么
`What` did you write in your IELTS essay?

### which one...
`Which` of these answers is correct?

## 第一人称

We should believe in `ourselves` during exams.

## 第二人称
我毛概考不好我就是国民党

You should believe in `yourself` more.

You all prepared the project by `yourselves`.
## 第三人称

He is very proud of `himself` for passing the exam.

She hurt `herself` while practicing sports.

The robot can operate by `itself`.

They prepared the presentation all by `themselves`.

## 作主语
### 近指
`This` is my IELTS practice notebook.

`These` are the grammar rules we reviewed yesterday.

### 远指
`That` was a great point you made in class.

`Those` were the best years of my life.

## 代指前文或后文
Many students rely on rote memorization. `This` often leads to poor critical thinking skills.
> 指刚刚提到的整件事、观点、现象，且你想强调它、承接它、进一步讨论它。

Some people believe AI will replace teachers. `That` is unlikely in the near future.
> 指前文提到的某事，但你把它当作一个已完成的事实、一个客观对象，或略带距离感/批评

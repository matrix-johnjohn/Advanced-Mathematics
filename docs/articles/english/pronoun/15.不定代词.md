## 表示TF逻辑

### 两者都有

`Both of` the students passed the IELTS exam.

`Both` grammar and `vocabulary` are essential for IELTS writing.

### 两者中的一个

`Either of` the two answers is correct.

`Either` my brother `or` I am responsible for this mistake.

### 两者都不
`Neither of` the two candidates is qualified.

`Neither` my brother `nor` I am good at politics.

### 多者都有
`All of` my friends are preparing for the entrance exam.

### 多者都不
`None of` the students has finished the assignment.

## 表示数量

### 指代或修饰可数名词复数
`Few` students understand this grammar rule.

I have `a few` questions about this topic.

`Many` factors affect language learning.

### 指代或修饰不可数名词复数
There is `little` time left to prepare for the exam.

I have `a little` experience with IELTS writing.

Is there `much` difference between these two methods?

## 表示其他
### 其他
Do you have other `questions`?

### 剩下的全部
I have two pens. One is red, and `the other` is blue.

Five students passed the exam. `The other students` failed.

Ten students came to class. Three were late; `the others` were on time.

### 剩下的多个
This cake is delicious. Can I have `another`?

I need `another` two days to finish the report.

Some workers are typing,and `others` are on the phone.


## 表达每一个
### 强调单个
`Each` student has a grammar notebook.

### 强调整体
`Every` candidate needs to prepare thoroughly.


## 复合不定代词

### Some
I need `someone` to help me with this grammar.

Would you like `something` to drink?

### Any
Did you see `anyone` at the library?

She doesn’t know `anything` about IELTS.

### Every
`Everyone` in our class passed the mock exam.

`Everything` is ready for the presentation.

### No
`Nobody` knows the answer to that question.

There is `nothing` wrong with your essay.

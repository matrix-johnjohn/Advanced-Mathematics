## 主语成分

### 第一人称
`I` am preparing for the IELTS exam.<br>

`We` study English together every evening.

### 第二人称
`You` should review grammar regularly.<br>

`You` are doing a great job preparing for the exams.

### 第三人称
`He` is preparing for the postgraduate entrance exam. <br>

`She` is applying for Guangdong’s special undergraduate transfer program. <br>

`They` passed the entrance exam last year.

## 宾语成分

### 第一人称
My teacher praised `me` for my writing improvement.<br>

The tutor helped `us` with essay structure.

### 第二人称
Can I give `you` some advice?<br>

I really admire `you` for your hard work.

### 第三人称
I saw `him` at the library yesterday.<br>

The scholarship was awarded to `her`.<br>

The professor invited `them` to join the research group.

## it的用法

### 指代
I bought a new laptop. `It` works very fast.<br>
> 指代前文提到的事物或刚提到的内容<br>

The exam was difficult, but `it` wasn’t impossible.

### 形式主语
`It` is important `to review grammar daily`.<br>

`It` is a fact `that English is widely used`.

### 形式宾语
I find `it` hard `to concentrate in noisy places`.<br>

She made `it` clear `that she won’t attend`.

### 无人称
`It` is 9 o’clock. 
> 表示时间

`It` is raining heavily.
> 表示天气

`It` is only 10 minutes from here to the library.
> 表示距离

### 强调句型
`It was` my English teacher `who` helped me improve my writing.<br>

`It is` consistent practice `that` leads to success in IELTS.

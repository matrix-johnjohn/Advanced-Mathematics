## 表示内容增补关系
### and
Tom `and` Jerry are friends.

### both...and...
`Both` Li Hua `and` Wang Fang are top students.

### neither...nor...
`Neither` the teacher `nor` the students know the answer.

### not only...but also
`Not only` the students `but also` the teacher is excited.

### as well as
The teacher, `as well as` his students, is preparing for the exam.

## 表示转折对比关系
### but
She studied hard, `but` she failed the exam.

### yet
He is young, `yet` he has great experience.

### while
`While` Guangzhou is hot, Harbin is freezing in winter.

### whereas
`Whereas` Theory A focuses on grammar, Theory B emphasizes communication.

### not...but...
The problem is `not` lack of time, `but` poor planning.

## 表示选择关系
### or
Would you like tea `or` coffee?

### or else
Stop talking, `or else` I’ll ask you to leave.

### either...or...
`Either` you apologize, `or` I’ll report you.

### rather than
I prefer walking `rather than` driving.

## 表示因果关系

### 提供推理性的原因
He must be tired, `for` he worked all night.

### 直接引出结果或结论
It was raining heavily, `so` we canceled the picnic.
